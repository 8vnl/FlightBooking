About TravelGo

TravelGo is a fictional booking system created as an individual project. It is designed to help users explore and book travel options with ease and convenience.

Our Mission

To provide a user-friendly and efficient booking platform that simplifies travel planning for everyone.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a reliable and innovative travel booking system that meets the needs of modern travelers.
